Our Story

Nandurbar's First Authorised MKCL Learning Centre

ITCT Computer Education was established in 2002 as the first Authorised Learning Centre (ALC) for MKCL in Nandurbar. Over two decades, we have been at the forefront of digital literacy education in this region, helping thousands of students, professionals, and homemakers acquire essential computer skills.

As an Authorised Learning Centre for both MS-CIT and KLiC courses under Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited (MKCL) — a Government of Maharashtra initiative — we deliver certified, quality education using MKCL's world-class ERA e-learning platform.

Our institute has grown from a single course offering to a comprehensive digital skills hub, catering to learners of all age groups. We take pride in being not just a computer institute, but a community partner in Nandurbar's journey toward digital empowerment.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

2001

MKCL & MS-CIT Launched

Maharashtra Knowledge Corporation Limited launches MS-CIT, Maharashtra's landmark IT literacy initiative.

2002

ITCT Established – First ALC in Nandurbar

ITCT Computer Education becomes the first Authorised Learning Centre for MKCL in Nandurbar, pioneering digital literacy in the region.

2010+

Growing Reputation & Expansion

Thousands of students trained, modern lab infrastructure added, batch sizes and course quality improved significantly.

2016+

KLiC Courses Added

ITCT becomes an Authorised KLiC ALC, offering career-oriented courses across multiple sectors under the MKCL-YCMOU framework.

2024–25

AI & Future-Ready Curriculum

MS-CIT's updated syllabus with AI tools is introduced; ITCT embraces the next generation of digital education.
