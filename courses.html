MKCL Flagship Course

MS-CIT

Maharashtra State Certificate in Information Technology — Maharashtra's most popular IT literacy course, started by MKCL in 2001 to help citizens develop essential digital skills in English, Marathi, or Hindi.

Course Overview

MS-CIT is an IT literacy course that equips learners with fundamental digital skills needed in personal, professional, and civic life. The updated curriculum now includes AI tools, empowering students to stay future-ready in the digital world.

Eligibility

Open to anyone above age 10 who can read and write. Preferably 5th Std. pass. No strict educational qualifications — accessible to students, professionals, homemakers, and senior citizens alike.

MKCL Career Courses

KLiC Courses

Knowledge Lit Career (KLiC) — A bridge from college to career. Industry-aligned, skill-focused certificate courses available in 120 hours and 60 hours, certified by YCMOU for 120-hour programmes.

About KLiC

KLiC courses are curated by industry experts, mapped to real workplace roles, and delivered through simulation-based, hands-on learning on MKCL's ERA platform. Courses are available across 11 sectors.

KLiC Diploma: Complete any 3 KLiC courses (120-hour each) within 1 year to earn a KLiC Diploma Certificate along with individual course certificates. Placement assistance is provided at no extra charge.
